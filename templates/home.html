Welcome

Pest Patrol is designed to protect crops with IoT-powered detection, solar energy for sustainability, and eco-friendly organic repellents. It automatically monitors fields, repels pests, and ensures higher yields while reducing the use of harmful chemicals.

Using smart sensors and data analytics, Pest Patrol identifies pest activity early, minimizing crop loss and maximizing farmer efficiency.

The system integrates seamlessly with solar-powered automation for year-round, self-sustaining operation in rural environments.

User Manual

1. Power on the system and ensure the solar panels are unobstructed.

2. Connect the IoT sensors to your network via the mobile app.

3. Monitor detected pests in real-time through the dashboard.

4. Activate pest repellent system when needed, either manually or automatically.

5. Review reports and historical pest data weekly to optimize crop protection.

6. Regularly clean sensors and solar panels for consistent performance.
