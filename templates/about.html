Pest Patrol

Pest Patrol is a multifunctional pest management system powered by IoT, solar energy, and organic solutions to provide eco-friendly and automated control of pests in crop fields. The system aims to reduce crop damage, increase productivity, and support sustainable farming practices.

Our platform combines real-time pest detection, automated pest control mechanisms, and data analytics to give farmers a comprehensive overview of their crop health. Pest Patrol is designed to be user-friendly, energy-efficient, and adaptable to various farm sizes.
